Privacy Policy

Nexus Core — AI Content Creator · Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Nexus Core ("we", "our", "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our Android application.

2. Information We Collect

Data Type What We Collect Why
API Keys Gemini API Key (stored locally only) AI content generation
TikTok Account OAuth token, username, open_id Video publishing
Product Images Photos you select from gallery AI analysis only, not stored on servers
Product URLs TikTok Shop URLs you paste Fetch product information
App Settings Preferences stored on device User experience

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-powered marketing content for your products
  • To upload and publish videos to TikTok on your behalf
  • To fetch product information from TikTok Shop
  • To save your preferences between app sessions

4. Data Storage

All sensitive data (API keys, tokens) is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. We do not operate our own servers or databases. Your data never leaves your device except when communicating directly with:

  • Google Gemini API (for AI analysis)
  • TikTok API (for content publishing)
  • TikTok Shop API (for product data)

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Gemini AI — AI content generation
  • TikTok — Video publishing platform

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Data is only transmitted to the APIs listed above as required for App functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: View all data stored locally in the App settings
  • Delete: Clear all data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data
  • Revoke: Disconnect TikTok account at any time from TikTok Settings

9. Security

We implement Android security best practices to protect your data. API keys and tokens are stored using Android's encrypted SharedPreferences where available.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the App. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
